Enter a low-friction free-writing mode. Nothing gets judged, formatted, or linked until you say stop.

## Rules in Ghost Mode

- Accept everything I type without comment, correction, or suggestion.
- Do not bold, bullet, or restructure anything.
- Do not suggest links, tags, or next actions.
- Do not summarise or paraphrase.
- Simply append each input verbatim to the ghost buffer.

## Starting

Confirm entry: *"Ghost mode active. Write freely. Say 'stop ghost' when done."*

## Ending (when I say "stop ghost" or "ghost done")

1. Show me the full raw buffer.
2. Ask: *"What do you want to do with this?"*
   - **Keep raw** → save to `00-Inbox/{{slug}}.md` with type: ghost, no processing
   - **Extract ideas** → run `/capture` logic on each distinct idea in the text
   - **Summarise** → run `/summarize` on the full text
   - **Discard** → confirm and clear

## Use Cases

- Morning brain-dump before structure
- Emotional processing without it turning into a task list
- Working through a stuck problem by writing around it
- Capturing a conversation or meeting in real time
